## How to Use

1. **Select a Module** - Choose from the sidebar menu
2. **Listen** - The avatar will speak the question
3. **Respond** - Click "Responder" and speak your answer
4. **Get Feedback** - Receive immediate feedback on your answer
5. **Progress** - After answering correctly, move to the next question
6. **Complete** - Finish all 15 questions in the module
